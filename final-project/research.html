Research

Welcome to the official research hub of the Paranormal Canine Research Collective (PCRC). Here, we investigate the strange and often unsettling intersections between canines and the unexplained. From untraceable growls in empty rooms to dogs reacting violently to unseen forces, our work is dedicated to collecting testimonies, conducting field investigations, and analyzing canine behaviors that defy rational understanding. Whether driven by instinct, energy sensitivity, or something beyond current science, dogs remain our most reliable witnesses to the supernatural.
